Complaints & Dispute Resolution

We take every complaint seriously. If we've fallen short of your expectations, tell us and we'll make it right — promptly, fairly, and transparently.

Acknowledged within 24 hours Resolved within 5–15 working days CBN Consumer Protection
How It Works

Our Complaint Resolution Process

A clear, transparent process from submission to resolution.

1

Submit Your Complaint

Use the form below, call our helpline, visit a branch, or email us. Provide as much detail as possible.

2

Acknowledgement

We'll acknowledge your complaint within 24 hours and issue a unique reference number.

3

Investigation

Our dedicated complaints team investigates and may contact you for additional information.

4

Resolution

We'll communicate our findings and resolution. If unsatisfied, you may escalate to the CBN.

Escalation to CBN

If your complaint is not resolved within our target timelines, or if you are unsatisfied with our resolution, you may escalate to the CBN Consumer Protection Department at cbn.gov.ng or call 0700 CALL CBN (0700 225 5226).
