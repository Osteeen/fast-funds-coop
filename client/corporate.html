Proof of Funds

Official Account Balance Confirmation

Fastfunds issues official Proof of Funds letters on our letterhead, signed and stamped by our management — accepted for visa applications, property purchases, government tenders, and business negotiations.

What is a Proof of Funds Letter?

A Proof of Funds (POF) letter is an official document issued by Fastfunds Cooperative confirming the balance in your account as at a specific date. It is stamped with our official seal and signed by a duly authorised officer of the society.

NGO Accounts

Accounts Designed for Non-Profits

Fastfunds understands the unique financial needs of NGOs, faith-based organisations, community associations, and clubs. Our NGO accounts provide full transparency, multi-signatory access, and compliance reporting.

Multi-Signatory Access

Configure approval hierarchies requiring 2-of-3 or any combination of signatories for transaction authorisation — ideal for committees and boards.

Fund Tracking

Track income and expenditure by fund, project, or donor. Generate reports for board meetings, audits, and donor accountability.

Donation Collection

Receive donations directly to your NGO account via USSD, bank transfer, or our payment link — fully auditable with donor records.

Compliance Reporting

Automated reports for SCUML compliance, annual returns, and donor/funder reporting requirements.

Requirements

  • CAC Incorporation or Trustees registration
  • Constitution / Trust Deed
  • Board/Trustee resolution to open account
  • Valid ID of all trustees/directors
  • SCUML registration certificate
  • Proof of office address

Benefits

  • No monthly maintenance fees for registered NGOs
  • Free quarterly account statements
  • Preferred processing for grant disbursements
  • Free cheque book for operational payments
