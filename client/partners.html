Grow Together With Fastfunds

We partner with employers, NGOs, financial institutions, community leaders, and referral agents to extend quality cooperative financial services to more Nigerians.

Why Partner

The Benefits of Partnering With Us

Fastfunds partnerships are built for mutual growth. We bring the financial infrastructure — you bring the community.

Revenue Sharing

Earn commissions and referral bonuses for every member you bring on board. Our incentive structure is transparent and rewarding.

Branded Financial Products

We co-create savings schemes, loan packages, and cooperative products tailored to your organisation's workforce or community.

Dedicated Support

Every partner gets a dedicated relationship manager, priority processing, and real-time reporting on your partnership performance.

Impact & Growth

Help your members, employees, and communities access real financial solutions — while growing your organisation's value and reputation.

Regulated & Trusted

Fastfunds operates under Nigerian cooperative law. Your brand is associated with a compliant, audited, and trusted financial institution.

Pan-African Reach

With members across Nigeria and the diaspora, partnering with Fastfunds connects you to a growing cross-border financial network.

Partnership Types

Find Your Partnership Model

Choose the partnership type that aligns with your organisation's goals.

Corporate & Employer Partnerships

We work with employers to bring payroll savings, emergency loans, and financial wellness benefits to their workforce at zero cost to the company.

  • Payroll-linked savings for staff
  • Emergency loan access for employees
  • Group cooperative membership
  • Financial literacy workshops

NGO & Community Partners

Partner with us to bring cooperative banking to underserved communities, market associations, religious groups, and grassroots cooperatives.

  • Micro-savings products for groups
  • Cooperative setup support
  • Community loan schemes
  • Agent banking integration

Referral Partners

Individuals and organisations can earn competitive commissions by referring customers who open accounts or take loans with Fastfunds.

  • Earn per successful referral
  • Real-time referral dashboard
  • Monthly commission payouts
  • No upfront investment needed

Financial Institution Partners

We welcome collaborations with banks, MFBs, fintechs, and insurance providers to co-create and cross-distribute financial products.

  • Product co-creation
  • Cross-referral agreements
  • API and data integration
  • Shared customer programmes
