FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does card delivery take?

Standard delivery to your registered address takes 5–7 working days. Branch pickup is available within 3 working days.

What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?

Immediately freeze your card through the Fastfunds app or call +234 800 FASTFUNDS. We will block the card and issue a replacement within 5 working days.

Can I use my Fastfunds card for international transactions?

International transactions are disabled by default for security. You can enable international use temporarily through the Fastfunds app before your trip and disable it when you return.

Are there card maintenance fees?

A card maintenance fee applies monthly as specified in our Tariff Schedule. The first year is free for new members.
