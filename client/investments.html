FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What investment products does Fastfunds offer?

We offer fixed-return cooperative investment pools (90–365 day tenors), share capital contributions (earning annual dividends), and tiered group investment schemes for members who want to pool funds for higher-tier access.

Is my investment guaranteed?

Principal is guaranteed on all Fastfunds investment products. Returns are fixed at subscription date and guaranteed for the agreed tenor. We do not offer equity-linked or variable-return products.

How are dividends calculated on shares?

Annual dividends are declared at the General Meeting based on the cooperative's audited profit for the year. Your dividend is proportional to your share capital holding relative to total shares outstanding.

Can I withdraw my investment before maturity?

Early withdrawal is subject to a penalty as specified in your investment agreement. We recommend aligning your investment tenor with your liquidity needs before committing.
