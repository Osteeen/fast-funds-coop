About Fastfunds

A member-driven cooperative society built on trust, transparency, and the collective pursuit of financial freedom.

Who We Are

Empowering Members,
Building Futures Together

Fastfunds Cooperative Society Limited is a member-driven financial institution committed to improving the economic wellbeing of our members across Nigeria. We provide access to affordable financial products, savings solutions, and business support services.

Founded on the principles of cooperation, mutual support, and shared prosperity, we have grown into a trusted financial partner for thousands of Nigerians — from individuals saving for their first car to businesses seeking growth capital.

Built on trust, transparency, and cooperation — our members are at the heart of every decision we make. Together, we are building a stronger financial future for every Nigerian.

Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses across Nigeria by providing accessible, affordable, and reliable financial products and services that improve livelihoods and drive economic growth within our communities.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and impactful cooperative financial institution in Nigeria — a place where every member has the tools, support, and opportunity to achieve lasting financial freedom and prosperity.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Trust

We earn and maintain the trust of our members through consistent honesty, fairness, and accountability in all we do.

Cooperation

We believe in the power of collective action. Our members grow together — each one's success strengthens the whole.

Transparency

We operate with full openness in our processes, fees, and decisions. No hidden charges. No surprises.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of service delivery, continuously improving to exceed member expectations.

Inclusion

Financial services should be accessible to everyone. We design products that serve members at every income level.

Integrity

We do what is right, even when no one is watching. Our members' financial wellbeing is always our first priority.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small savings circle to a fully-fledged cooperative financial institution — our growth has always been fueled by the trust of our members.

Founded

The Beginning

Fastfunds was established as a small cooperative savings group by a community of professionals committed to helping each other achieve financial goals.

Growth

Expanding Our Products

We introduced our first formal loan product and fixed deposit scheme, growing our membership base significantly across Lagos.

Digital

Going Digital

Launch of our internet banking portal and mobile app, enabling members to access services from anywhere in Nigeria.

Today

A Trusted Institution

With 10,000+ active members, ₦2B+ in loans disbursed, and 15+ products, Fastfunds is one of Nigeria's most trusted cooperative societies.
