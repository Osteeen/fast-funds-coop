Membership

Becoming a member is easy. You can sign up online through our website or visit any of our offices. You'll need a valid government-issued ID, your BVN, a passport photograph, and proof of address. Membership is open to all Nigerian adults aged 18 and above.

There is a one-time membership registration fee. Please visit your nearest Fastfunds office or contact us for current fee details. This fee gives you access to all our financial products and member benefits.

Currently, Fastfunds membership is open to Nigerian citizens and legal residents with valid Nigerian BVN and identification. Foreign nationals residing in Nigeria may apply subject to additional verification requirements.

Accounts

For a personal account you need: valid government-issued ID (NIN, National Passport, or Driver's Licence), your BVN, a recent passport photograph, and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement not older than 3 months). For business accounts, additional CAC documents are required.

Online account opening is instant — you receive your account number within minutes of completing your application. In-branch account opening typically takes 30–60 minutes depending on document verification.

Our savings account has no minimum balance requirement. Fixed deposit and investment accounts have minimum balance requirements which vary by product. Please see the products page for specific details.

Yes. Members can hold multiple account types simultaneously — for example, a savings account, a fixed deposit, and a business current account — all under the same membership profile.

Loans

To qualify for a loan you must be an active Fastfunds member, have a funded account, and not have any outstanding loan with us. First-time borrowers are eligible for our Tier 1 loan limit. Your limit increases as you build a repayment history with us.

Our personal loans are charged at a flat interest rate set by management — currently 5% per month on the principal amount. This rate applies for the duration of your loan and is fixed at the time of approval. Changes to the interest rate only affect new loan applications.

Most loan applications are reviewed within 24–48 business hours. Disbursement occurs on the same day as approval. Complex applications requiring additional documentation may take longer.

Yes. You can repay your loan partially or in full before the due date. Early repayment may attract a small fee depending on your loan type. Please contact us before making an early repayment to get your outstanding balance and any applicable charges.

If your account does not have sufficient funds on the repayment due date, the debit will still be attempted and your account may go into a negative balance. Missed repayments are flagged and may affect your eligibility for future loans. Please fund your account before due dates to avoid this.

No. Our policy allows only one active loan per member at a time. You must fully repay your current loan before applying for another. This policy protects both members and the cooperative from over-indebtedness.

Savings & Deposits

Our savings account currently offers competitive interest rates. Fixed deposit rates are higher and vary by tenor. Please check our products page or contact us for current rates as these are subject to change.

Early withdrawal from a fixed deposit is possible but may result in a reduction of your accrued interest. The exact penalty depends on how far from maturity your withdrawal is. We recommend contacting us to understand the implications before proceeding.

You set a savings goal and a target date, then make regular contributions toward it. The account is designed to restrict withdrawals until the goal is met or the target date is reached, helping you stay disciplined. You earn interest throughout the savings period.

Cards

You can request a debit card through the mobile app, internet banking portal, or by visiting any Fastfunds office. Cards are typically ready for collection within 3–5 working days or can be delivered to your registered address.

Immediately block your card through the mobile app or internet banking portal under "Card Management." Then call our 24/7 support line to report the loss and request a replacement card. A replacement fee may apply.

Virtual card generation may attract a small fee depending on card type (NGN or USD). Details are available in the mobile app. The card is instant and ideal for online shopping and subscriptions.

Digital Banking

Visit our internet banking portal and click "Register." You'll need your account number, registered phone number, and a valid email address. An OTP will be sent to verify your details, after which you can set your login credentials.

Dial *000# from any mobile network to access Fastfunds USSD banking. You can check your balance, transfer funds, buy airtime, and pay bills — all without internet access.

Click "Forgot Password" on the internet banking login page. Enter your registered email address or phone number and follow the OTP verification steps to reset your password. If you encounter any issues, contact our support team.

Security

We use bank-grade encryption for all transactions, OTP verification for sensitive operations, and real-time fraud monitoring. We will never ask for your PIN, password, or OTP via phone or email. Always transact on our official platforms only.

Immediately call our 24/7 helpline or send an email to info@fastfunds.coop. Include your account number, the suspicious transaction details, date, and amount. Our security team will investigate and respond within 2 hours for urgent cases.

Yes. Fastfunds is a registered cooperative society operating under Nigerian law and financial regulations. Member funds are managed responsibly with proper governance structures, independent audits, and regulatory oversight.
